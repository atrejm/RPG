Part 56. Design a Moment:

1. Overview - One sentence
        Player has to break into the church to rescue a priestess that has been taken hostage

2. Where
        In the small village at the outskirts of a kingdom

3. What is player trying to do
        Rescuing a priestess from the clutches of a gang of hired mercenaries. She is locked up in
        in the back room of the town church, with a number of guards patrolling the front of the 
        building.


4. One meaningful choice you can give player
        The player can either brute force in through the front door, or find a key to sneak in
        through a side entrance
